Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a grassroots meetup of tech professionals passionate about innovation and community. Over the years, it has grown into a major industry event attracting thousands from around the globe.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to empower the global tech community by fostering innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning. We believe in a future built by inclusive, forward-thinking minds.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Maria Lopez

AI ethics advocate and professor at MIT. Dr. Lopez delivered a keynote on responsible machine learning practices at TechCon 2022.

James Okoye

Founder of GreenStack and a pioneer in sustainable cloud infrastructure. He spoke on energy-efficient computing at TechCon 2021.
